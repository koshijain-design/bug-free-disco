Oxygen (O)

Atomic Number: 8
Atomic Mass: 15.999 u
Properties: Essential gas for respiration.
Uses: Breathing, welding, hospitals, steel making.
Benefits: Supports life and metabolic processes.

How Humans Overuse This Element

Heavy industrial demand increases energy consumption.

Environmental Harm

Air separation plants consume large amounts of electricity.

Sustainable Alternatives

Use renewable-energy-powered oxygen plants.
